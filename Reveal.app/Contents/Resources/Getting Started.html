Welcome to Reveal 1.6

What's New?

  • Multi-window support: Get different perspectives of your app or inspect multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Create and share snapshots: Share snapshots with your team or use them as point-in-time reference during development.
  • iOS 9 support: Integrate Reveal with bitcode enabled applications and inspect new classes and properties in iOS 9.
  • Inspect tvOS apps: Seamlessly inspect tvOS apps using the same powerful tools you use for iOS apps.
  • Reveal now requires OS X 10.10 "Yosemite" or newer to run. We've also bumped up the minimum iOS version to iOS 7.
  • Many bug fixes, performance improvements, and a clean-up of the user interface.
